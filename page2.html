Professional Career

Neymar made his professional debut with Santos in 2009 and won the Copa Libertadores in 2011, scoring in the final and game and then leaving his childhood club to go and play for spanish side Barcelona

Neymar won the treble of La Liga, the Copa del Rey, and the Champions League, and in that season many people consider it being his prime season as he finished the season being the top goalscorer of both that season's Champions League and the Copa del Rey.

Neymar then left barcelona and went to Paris Saint-German in a breaking transfer of €222 million, he was not at his best form during his time at PSG due to injuries keeping coming onto him, and in 2023 he decided to leave PSG and go to Al Hilal.

Neymar did not have a good time in Al Hilal, as the injury problem was not solved and he barely even played, and for the two years he stayed there he only played 3 games and finally he decided to move back to his childhood club, Santos.

Neymar is doing pretty good right now for Santos, as he helped them avoid relegation, played 32 games, and scored 14 goals and still counting.
