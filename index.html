Neymar Da Silva Santos Junior was born on febrary 5th, 1992 in Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, Brazil.

Neymar gre up in poverty, but his love for the game didn't stop and he still fought threw them.

As any Brazilian young kid with a dream, Neymar loved plaing futsul and street football, and he said that these moments were the moments that made him what he is.

In 2003 his family moved to Santos and he went and started playing for the Club Santos, and at 15 he was earning enough for his family to go out of poverty and actually buy a decent property.
